What is Leaf?


Leaf is a mission-driven not-for-profit organization that connects students to high school success and opportunity.

Founded in 2014, Leaf was created to expand access to secondary education. Today, the membership association is made up of over 3 of Stuyvesant High School's mediocre software developers, dedicated to promoting excellence and equity in education.

Tianshi Wang: Open Data Analysis
(Github manager and leader, owner of droplet)
Brian Guo: Code Supervisor
(API back end, owner of server)
Chris Kim: Coder, Issues Manager, Communications
(Login and register back end, owner of domain)
David Liang: daVid
(Javascript and HTML front end)

Leaf helps students research NYC public high schools, using the public information from NYC OpenData. Register and log in with your account to explore high school trees. You can find useful information about each school on their profiles, and rate your favorite high schools!
